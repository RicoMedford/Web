Cloudiness

This chart above is trying to show if there is a relationship with cloudiness and city latitude. However, looking at the plot you can see it is scattered and doesn't show a trend.

Fun Fact: Different types of clouds can be found at different heights in the sky. In addition to cloud type determining its height, latitude plays a role in how high a cloud is in the sky. Most clouds we see, including clouds that are related to weather, are located in a layer of the atmosphere called the troposphere.

Reference: Becca Hatheway "Cloud Heights at Different Latitudes"
