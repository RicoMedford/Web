Humidity

The chart above is trying to show if there is a direct correlation between humidity and city latitude. Based on this chart we can determine that there is not a signifcant relationship between humidity and latitude.

Fun Fact: Relative humidity is stated as a percentage. The closer relative humidity gets to 100 percent, the more likely it is that clouds form. Dew point is the temperature to which air must be cooled to become saturated with water vapor. The closer air temperature gets to the dew point, the more likely it is that clouds form.

Reference: Nathan Chandler "What is relative humidity"
